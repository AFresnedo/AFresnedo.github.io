What is this page?

I built this page because I wanted to have a place to jot down my values, opinions, musings, and takeaways from my experience as a developer. My hope is that this page is indicitive of what talking to me about these topics is like. It should also serve as a quick review of what I know (or at least have been exposed to).

Math Affirm

Math Affirm allows teachers to create instructional theory files, problems, interfaces for answers, solutions, and the hints related to each solution. Students progress through problems, according to the route defined by the teacher, and have their scores recorded.

  • placeTechnologies
    • Ruby on Rails
    • Python
    • Bootstrap 3
  • placeFeatures
    • Individual Student Records
    • Offline Input Tools for Problem-related Content
    • Visual Representations of Curriculum
    • Flexible Pathing Through Curriculum

Park Owl

Park Owl is designed to give park goers easy access to notify their local parks department of maintainance and accessibility issues. The key feature being currently developed will allow users to reach out to local government representatives, in an effort to support their parks department.

  • placeTechnologies
    • Express
  • placeFeatures

    • Camera Snapshot Uploads
    • Geolocation for Park Information
    • Local Representatives Discovery
    • Park Issue Submission to Local Parks Department
    • Administrative Interface for Parks Department to Handle Issues
